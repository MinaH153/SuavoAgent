# Pharmacy Cold Call Script

## Opening (15 seconds)

"Hi, this is Joshua from Suavo. We're a local prescription delivery service. I'm reaching out to independent pharmacies in the area — do you currently offer delivery to your patients?"

**If YES:** "Great — how are you handling the pickup coordination? Like, how does the driver know when prescriptions are actually ready?"

**If NO:** "That's actually why I'm calling. We handle the entire delivery process — drivers, routing, everything. The pharmacist doesn't have to change anything about their workflow."

## The Hook (30 seconds)

"Here's what makes us different from other delivery services: we have a small piece of software that sits on your pharmacy computer and watches PioneerRx [or their PMS]. It learns when prescriptions are ready and automatically tells our drivers exactly when to show up. No more drivers waiting at the counter. No more your staff stopping to look things up."

## Common Objections

**"We already use [competitor] for delivery"**
"Totally get it. The difference is timing — [competitor] sends a driver when the order comes in. Our driver shows up when the bag is actually ready. That saves your staff 5-10 minutes per batch because nobody's standing around waiting."

**"I don't want software on my computer"**
"Completely understand. It's invisible — no windows, no pop-ups. It just reads what's already in [PMS name] to know when things are ready. We can show you exactly what it sees — it's all laid out in the disclosure document. And you can uninstall it any time with one phone call."

**"How much does it cost?"**
"Zero. The delivery service is how we make money. The software is free — it just makes our drivers show up at the right time."

**"What about HIPAA?"**
"Great question. We're a Business Associate — we sign a BAA. The software is HIPAA compliant, encrypted, and has been through a full security audit. It never captures patient names, SSNs, or any personal information. We can walk through the compliance docs whenever you're ready."

**"I need to think about it"**
"Of course. Can I drop off a one-pager and the compliance docs? That way you have everything in front of you. I'll follow up [day] — what time works best?"

## The Close

"Would it make sense for me to stop by this week and show you how it works? It takes about 5 minutes to install and you'll see results on the first delivery batch."

## After the Call

- [ ] Log pharmacy name, contact, PMS system, current delivery setup
- [ ] Send one-pager + compliance docs via email or drop off
- [ ] Schedule follow-up within 48 hours
- [ ] Note any specific objections or concerns for next call
