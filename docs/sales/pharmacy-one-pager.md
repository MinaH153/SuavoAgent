# Suavo — Prescription Delivery That Times Itself

## The Problem

Every delivery service sends a driver when the order comes in. Your staff stops what they're doing to check if it's ready. The driver waits. Your pharmacist loses 5-10 minutes per batch.

## How Suavo Works

1. **You fill prescriptions** — nothing changes about your workflow
2. **Our software watches PioneerRx** — it knows when prescriptions move to delivery-ready status
3. **We dispatch the driver at the right moment** — the driver arrives when the bag is actually ready
4. **Delivery proof writes back** — status updates automatically in your PMS

## What Makes Us Different

| | Traditional Delivery | Suavo |
|---|---|---|
| Driver dispatch | When order placed | When prescription is ready |
| Staff interruption | Every pickup | Zero |
| Status updates | Manual | Automatic |
| Setup time | Training + workflow change | 5-minute install, no training |
